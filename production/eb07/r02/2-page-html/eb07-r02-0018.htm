A series of important experiments were made by our author in order to ascertain the relation between the velocity at the surface of any stream and that at the bottom. Hence we learn that this velocity of separation is in all cases indefinitely small, and that we may, without danger of any sensible error, suppose it a constant quantity in all cases.
