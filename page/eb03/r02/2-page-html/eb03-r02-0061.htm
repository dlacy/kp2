But it will afford a moſt •agreeable object to a conſiderate and contemplative mind, to take it up in this very ſimple light ; and having conſidered the many ways in which the drenched ſurface might have been cleared of the ſuperfluous waters, to attend particularly to the very way which nature has followed.
