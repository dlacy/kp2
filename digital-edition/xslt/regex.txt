regex for identifying multiple footnotes on the same line of TEI files:

FIND:
([\.|"])[\s|\h]+@@@

REPLACE:
\1</p><p>@@@

(NOTE: \h refers to a "horizontal space," which is used by Oxygen but not included in \s.)

CORRECT EXTRA SPACES AROUND <hi> tags

F:
\s+<hi\srend=("\w+")>\s+
R:
 <hi rend=\1>
 
CORRECT ENTITIES

f:	&amp;([a-z]{2,3};)
r:	&\1
